• Makeup Tips
    • Foundation
      1. Choose the right shade for your skin tone.
      2. Blend foundation well into your jawline and neck.
      3. Consider your skin type when selecting a foundation formula.
    • Eyeshadow
      1. Use primer to create a smooth base for eyeshadow.
      2. Blend multiple eyeshadow colors for a seamless look.
      3. Highlight the inner corners of your eyes to make them pop.
    • Lip Color
      1. Exfoliate your lips before applying lipstick for a smoother finish.
      2. Use a lip liner to define the shape of your lips.
    • Skincare Prep
      1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.
      2. Apply sunscreen to protect your skin from UV rays.
      3. Use a hydrating primer for a dewy makeup look.
    • Tools and Techniques
      1. Invest in quality makeup brushes for precise application.
